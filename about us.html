Name and Purpose

1.1 NAME
The name of the organization is "JESUS CARES CHARITY" with its offices based in Monrovia, Republic of Liberia.
"JESUS CARES CHARITY" (CC) is the acronymn of Jesus Children Aid, Relief & Educational Services, a humanitarian arm of Trinity Outreach Ministries in Liberia, West Africa.
The motto is: Help us to help the world.

1.2 OBJECTIVE
Jesus Cares Charity is the relief and humanitarian arm of Trinity Outreach Ministries Inc, established for the objects as follows: 1. To contribute immensely and critically to the full and holistic development of children in Liberia.
2.To provide protection and advocacy for children against all forms of violence and abuse including but not limited to sexual exploitation, child trafficking, and substance and drug abuse.
3. To ensure that the children of Liberia participate and benefit from the accomplishment of the Millennium Development Goals (MDGS) by 2050, thru education, awareness, campaign, project implementation, etc.
4. To help provide food, clothes and shelters for orphans, under-privileged children and poor-families in slum communities.
5.To mobilize resources for the establishment of health and educational facilities, such as schools, clinics, hospitals, recreational centers, etc, for the wellbeing of orphans and under-privileged children.

1.2 VISION
To ensure that orphans and underprivileged children have a better living standard to reach their full potentials in life.

1.3 MISSION STATEMENT
To represent the interest and aspiration of orphans, the under-privileged children and their poor-families in Liberia and the world at large, and to restore hope among vulnerable children and communities that have been affected by poverty, violence, abuse, disease and other natural calamities.
