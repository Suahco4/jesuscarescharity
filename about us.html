About Our Organization

Learn more about our purpose, mission, and the change we are creating.

Our Identity and Purpose

The name of the organization is "JESUS CARES CHARITY" (JCC), the humanitarian arm of Trinity Outreach Ministries in Liberia, West Africa. Our motto is: "Help us to help the world."

Our Vision

To ensure that orphans and underprivileged children have a better living standard to reach their full potentials in life.

Our Mission

To represent the interest and aspiration of orphans, the under-privileged children and their poor-families in Liberia and the world at large, and to restore hope among vulnerable communities affected by poverty, violence, abuse, and disease.

Our Core Objectives

Jesus Cares Charity was established with the following key objectives:

  • To contribute immensely to the full and holistic development of children in Liberia.
  • To provide protection and advocacy for children against all forms of violence and abuse, including sexual exploitation, child trafficking, and substance abuse.
  • To ensure that the children of Liberia participate in and benefit from the accomplishment of the Millennium Development Goals (MDGs) through education, awareness, and project implementation.
  • To help provide food, clothes, and shelter for orphans, under-privileged children, and poor families in slum communities.
  • To mobilize resources for establishing health and educational facilities, such as schools, clinics, and recreational centers, for the wellbeing of vulnerable children.
