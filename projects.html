School Project in Bomi County

    Launching a new school project to bring education to the children of Taquoi town, Bomi county. Our goal is to provide quality education and resources to empower the youth in this community.

    Help us keep smiles on the faces of these kids as we plan to construct schools, provide learning materials, and support their educational journey.

    The church building is currently being used as a classroom to teach the children in the community, while we plan to construct a proper school building.

    This church building is hosting six classes for both nursery and elementary levels.

    Rev. Lyndon Jaesen is the principal of the school and the pastor for the church. He is asking for help to construct a proper school building for the children in the community.

    The kids are eager to learn and are very grateful for the opportunity to attend school. can you help us complete the project?
